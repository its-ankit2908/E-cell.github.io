Idea-Box


Entrepreneurship Cell (E-CELL) of Medi-Caps University organized a campus-wide activity with a call for ideas. It involved placing 5 boxes at various places inside the university campus with the aim to urge the students to present their ideas or start-up plans to the Faculty Co-ordinators of E-cell . This initiative was taken to bring the potential ideas or existing start-ups of the students of the university to light and to help them grow by providing them the required support.

Of all the unique ideas collected in a span of 3 days of the campaign, top 10 were selected for final presentation.

The 2 best conceptions of Idea Box were "The Bookchain", based on Block Chain and “Let Her Travel” which encourages women to explore and travel by organising women-only trips.
