Entrepreneurs-drop-in


E-Cell Medi-Caps University organised “Entrepreneur’s Drop In” ,graced by the revered Mr. Siddharth Rajhans. The keynote speaker shared his journey and gave insights into his work, thereby, inspiring the spectators.


A paragon of multi-tasking, Mr. Rajhans is a Product Manager at Apple, currently pursuing his executive MBA degree from Harard University and running “Spacify”, his start-up. He addressed the necessity of constant innovation for sustenance of a company and fortified the students to set up their own business.
