What's my story


The Entrepreneurship Cell of Medi-Caps University conducted an event “What’s My Story?” where we had three successful young entrepreneurs who inspired and guided the enthusiastic audience with their start-up stories.

The impresarios, Mr. Nehul Agrawal who is man of action and founder of foduu.com, Mr. Saurabh Shrivastava who is founder of Mangosteen Eco Lifestyle and an award winner for International Quality Certification by WSC, UK and Mr. Anurag Agrawal who founded jawabdo.co.in. Mr.Vikas Singh, who is mentor at Srijan incubation centre by AKVN crystal IT park, Indore accompanied the rest at the panel and interacted with the students. The speakers shared their journey and motivated the students.
