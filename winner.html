Let Her Travel

E-cell conducted a contest "Idea Box", where we placed boxes at various locations in our campus and the students could insert their ideas in the box which they wish to turn into a startup. At the end of the "Idea Box" activity, we evaluated entries and declared a startup idea "Let Her Travel" as the winner and helped them convert their idea into reality.
Let Her Travel is still operating. The idea behind this startup was to revolutionize the way we think about women travelling in our developing country. They createdservices that ensured that women can enjoy their freedom of exploring places. The services were security guards, professional drivers and guides.
