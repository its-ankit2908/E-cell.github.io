Let Her Travel

Let Her Travel is a beautiful initiative taken by young entrepreneurs in order to facilitate travel among women. It is the first women centered organization which plans, customizes and organizes trips only for women. Let her travel was formed in 2019 with a vision to redefine boundaries for women and evolutionize the way female travelling is perceived in the developing country. It is driven by a mission to spread happiness and turn all the travelling fantasies of women come true. It inspires women to wander the world without limitations and makes travel safer for them. It has conducted many trips for women across the nation, has capacitated over 200 women rovers to spread their wings and fly-high and continues to do so with same zeal and ardour. E-Cell has been a proud supporter of Let her travel, working firmly towards their belief of helping budding entrepreneur go through the journey of converting ideas into reality.

Egniter

Egniter has carved a niche for itself in the Event Management, Marketing and Designing circuits in central India. It has become a celebrated name amongst the aspirants of management related activities. It holistically promotes excellence, Intellectual discipline, and professional leadership. Egniter is a Start-up formed in 2019 with the motive to help businesses emerge and connect to the creative world, building the perfect avenue for one’s brand to lead. A team that strives towards creating an ecosystem to provide the services such as designing, marketing, corporate as well as outdoor events to various companies with creative minds and ideas and potential for substantial business growth. At Egniter, while excellence and professionalism are meant to be uncompromising, it is believed that the significance of entrepreneurial skill is indelible to an integrated experience in various communities. E-Cell has been a strong part of Egniter’s journey into reality. The founder of Egniter is currently the president of E-Cell and is helping further in shaping fledging minds to become future inventorial entrepreneurs.
